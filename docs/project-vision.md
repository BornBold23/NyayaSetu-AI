# NyayaSetu AI

## Vision

NyayaSetu AI is an AI-powered Crime Awareness, Community Reporting and Case Intelligence Platform built for India.

The platform aims to educate citizens, improve public awareness, encourage anonymous reporting, provide AI-generated case summaries, and present crime analytics through interactive dashboards.

## Mission

To make crime-related information accessible, organized and useful while promoting public awareness and responsible community participation.

## Objectives

- Increase crime awareness.
- Help citizens understand different crime types.
- Allow anonymous reporting.
- Generate AI summaries.
- Build crime analytics dashboards.
- Educate people on safety measures.
